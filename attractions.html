Attractions

Taniti Beach

Relax on the pristine sands of Taniti Beach, perfect for sunbathing and swimming.

Taniti Waterfalls

Explore the stunning waterfalls hidden in the lush jungles of Taniti.

Taniti Volcano

Hike to the summit of Taniti Volcano for breathtaking views of the island.

Taniti Cultural Village

Experience the rich culture and traditions of Taniti at the Cultural Village.

Taniti Marine Park

Dive into the vibrant underwater world of Taniti Marine Park, home to diverse marine life.

Taniti Night Market

Discover local crafts, food, and entertainment at the bustling Taniti Night Market.

Taniti Hiking Trails

Explore the scenic hiking trails that wind through Taniti's diverse landscapes.

Taniti Botanical Gardens

Stroll through the lush Taniti Botanical Gardens, showcasing native flora and fauna.

Taniti Art Gallery

Admire the works of local artists at the Taniti Art Gallery, featuring contemporary and traditional art.
